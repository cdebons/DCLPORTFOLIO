VIVI

Pradash Gardens

ETHZ ERASMUS SEMESTER 5 - 2019

STUDIO LUDGER HOVESTADT

INDIVIDUAL WORK

“A substantial understanding of today’s technology (internet, big data, machine intelligence…) changes the perspective to architectural technology and will result in different architectural designs. […] Prada is dead. We move in to show precious pieces of nature and support projects of radical recycling.” – Prof. Ludger Hovestadt.​

Vivi, the brand created around this project, aims to give our bodies a second life. Inviting the living to choose which plant they want to become and where they want to be planted when they die.

Pradash gardens, Vivi's first shop, is a place where people bring the bodies of their loved ones and accompany them in their metamorphosis. The bodies are reduced to ashes, mixed wiht the chosen seeds and planted in a vase as the mourners cross the gardens.
