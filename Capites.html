CAPITES

3 Capties in Jogny

EPFL YEAR 2 - 2019

FIRE - FIiguration et Représentation de L'Architecture

GROUP WORK with Luana Ferrari & Chiara Kempter

Vernacular architecture is by definition: “characterised by its reliance on needs, construction materials and traditions specific to its particular locality”. This work of interpretation, shaping and appropriation of vineyard huts helped build a critical as well as analytical reading of cultural architecture. 3 huts situated in Jogny above Vevey were thoroughly analysed.
