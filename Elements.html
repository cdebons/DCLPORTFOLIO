ELEMENTS

Window-Table

EPFL YEAR 1 - 2017

STUDIO FRANCOIS NANTERMOD

GROUP WORK with Eva Hauswirth & Tristan Kramer

A site is given. The construction of an element starts. Two scissors held together by one central piece: the white key. This huge “statue like” machine is inspired by the massif pillars of the viaduct. Folded, it hides a mystery and invites the people passing by to come and open it. Unfolded, it is a place where one can come, put down their drink and look through its triangle windows that frame the flyover.
