ZOLLHUSBRUCKE

Zollhusbrücke Tüüffelschanze, Eschikofen

ETHZ MASTERS - SEMESTER 5 - 2022

PROF. DR. STEFAN HOLZER - Construction History and Building Preservation

GROUP WORK with Amélie Chiffelle, Céline Bourban & Manon Amsler

Zollhusbrücke Tüüffelschanze is a wooden bridge situated in Eschikofen, a municipality of the canton of Thurgau. It bridges the two shores of the river Thur and is considered the oldest road network structure in the canton of Thurgau.

The history of the bridge is highly related to the flooding of the river. This is reflected not only in the way the bridge is integrated in the landscape but also in its construction.

The aim of this work was to understand the static qualities of this historical bridge, the wooden construction and also the modifications throughout it’s history.
