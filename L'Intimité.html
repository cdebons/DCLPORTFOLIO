L'INTIMITE

ein Zustand tiefster persönliche Vertrauheit

ETHZ MASTER SEMESTER 2 - 2022

STUDIO ANGELA DEUBER

INDIVIDUAL WORK

ORIGINAL PROJECT LANGUAGE: GERMAN

Intimacy is unfathomable, it is expressed through a sense of loss of spatial scale as well as in a continuity of forms and structures. The building is located in Soho (242 Lafayette Street, 4th Floor, 10012 New York, USA, 1901); historically, a cultural centre where artists lived in old industrial buildings. Gentrification made it difficult for artists to afford these spaces. The project aims to bring back spaces for artists within Soho.

The project thus transforms the 104 square meter loft into two studios where the artists have their own living spaces, a common kitchen and an art studio. The intervention is minimalistic. One third of the space is left for the art studio, two vessels face each other forming the private rooms for the artists. These two vessels create a long corridor that leads to a common kitchen. The walls of the vessels integrate all the furniture.

These are made of laminated wooden profiles which are held together by horizontal wooden elements. Between this grid, insulation is inserted and then protected by wooden panels. Finally the panels are covered by a waterproof fabric.
