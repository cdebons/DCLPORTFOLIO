HOUSE III

The Breakfast Room

EPFL YEAR 1 - 2018

STUDIO FRANCOIS NANTERMOD

GROUP WORK with Eva Hauswirth, Tristan Kramer, Alicia Fankhauser, Evan Ribadeau Dumas, Fiona Pèpe, François Born, Gaëtan Détraz, Laura Guerreiro, Mathieu Stoppa, Meryl Barthe, Nicolas Pralong, San Yun & Zelal Sari

The protostructure of HOUSE III is a wooden structure whose construction is based on the orthogonal assembly of standardized wooden beams. Hence creating a three dimensional grid in which each studio created a project. This “house” was prefabricated in the first year studios at the EPFL and then hand built in Brussels in the KANAL museum. Studio Nantermod built the breakfast room consisting in a deck, an arched roof as well as a table. ​
